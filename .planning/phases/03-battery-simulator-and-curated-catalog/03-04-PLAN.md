This violates the SimResult invariant documented in types.ts:250-253 ("the honest what-would-remain totals") and undermines the project's core "honest comparison" value.

Output: A corrected `src/domain/simulate.ts` that preserves both real flows, and a new regression fixture in `tests/simulate.test.ts` that locks energy conservation on a both-nonzero interval.
